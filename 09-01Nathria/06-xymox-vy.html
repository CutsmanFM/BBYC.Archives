@Delta_Squad 🔺 The mythic mechanic for Artificer is easy to explain on paper, basically it's the same heroic mechanics except they continue on for the rest of the fight instead of changing every phase. So starting in phase 3 (which starts at 60% on mythic) you will have the annihilation pull in, mind control ghosts, and the seeds all at once, but they happen in different orders based on the annihilate cast number so it's important people know how to handle each individual overlap set. So here is how the annihilations (pull ins) will go: 1. Annihilation -> Seeds -> Ghosts 2. Immune overlap: Ghosts -> Annihilation -> Seeds 3. Traps -> Annihilation -> Seeds -> Ghosts The only RNG in the fight is whether you individually get a ghost or not. Ghost always spawn behind you though, so you can force ghosts to not spawn in a bad spot based on your positioning. If you are a hunter or mage you will likely be assigned to running seeds. So look at the two bad overlaps and how you would handle them if you had a ghost on you. Assume ice block/turtle is on cooldown The two overlaps are the First annihilation overlap (at 4:15 in the video) and the second annihilation overlap (at 5:25 in the video). Definitely check those out if nothing else. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EUrtxBnIjNw
