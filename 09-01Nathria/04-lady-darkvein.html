Here's some more context for the containers, which won't take long to learn. The new UI element corresponds to each container, top to bottom on the UI element corresponds to the four containers left to right. They gain energy passively over time and can be clicked to be emptied (at the expense of moderate raid damage). Letting one cap out is a wipe She focuses one container which means it can't be clicked/emptied until her focus is broken, and the focused container gains energy much faster than the others. breaking her focus happens automatically after 100 seconds but can also be done by dealing 25% of her hp. There are two general rules on when a container should be clicked: 1. Click the focused container right after her focus breaks, because it will be full 2. Click a container right before it is about to be focused so it doesn't cap out from the increased energy gain. We will let all containers reach level 3 except for the 3rd container, we want to click that before that reaches its 3rd level. So we may lust on the 3rd container depending on how our damage looks.
