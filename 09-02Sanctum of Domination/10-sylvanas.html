In preparation for Sylvanas there is only one mechanic I'd like everyone to look at ahead of time, which is the 3 wailing arrows. That is the mechanic we've seen on normal where 3 people get targeted for a big detonation and must run to the next platform to detonate as it is falloff damage for the entire raid. The tricky part is not going too early or too late so you still detonate on the next platform but aren't on that platform when the other wailing arrows detonate. The first one is on a tank and they will jump over early to do tank stuff. The timings on when the 2nd and 3rd person jump are somewhat precise, and it would be good if everyone understood how those work and how to react to the weakaura. So basically she puts a debuff on 3 people, which indicates they are targeted. Just before the debuff expires, she does a 1.5 sec cast, and at the end of the cast she shoots the wailing arrow which is a projectile that moves fast but still does have some travel time. There is 3 seconds in between detonations so you need to move back and forth quickly. The easiest rule of thumb is to jump over as the previous person's arrow is flying overhead. So if you're the 2nd arrow, wait for the 1st person's debuff bar to expires, watch for her cast targeting the 1st person, then move over when you see the projectile (again, if you're the 2nd person you're looking to react to the projectile targeting the 1st person). Here is a video example, it should start at 25:30. Just watch 5 mins of this vid, from 25:30 to 31:00 ish, that will show what the weakaura bars look like and what to look for in terms of the projectile flying overhead (edited)
