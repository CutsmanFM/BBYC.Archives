btw note for our hunters using wild spirits, you can use them on CD once you use it when you go down, but you need to be quick when recasting outside kingsmourne phases. The most important uses are during the down phases of course, and those are just barely more than 4:00 apart. This is especially true for group B hunters who are using their wild spirits on pull. Using on pull is prob fine but you go down at 1:45, and because its a 35 second phase that means you're forced to use your 2nd cast 15 seconds into the phase. That will still get full duration, but if you delay your 3rd cast at all that means your 4th cast (when you go down again) will be delayed and that will lose out on dps. So here are some timings: Group A (1st kingsmourne) WS timings 1st: 0:45 - start of 1st P1 kingsmourne (starts at 0:45) 2nd: 2:45 - 1st intermission 3rd: 4:45 - start of 1st P2 kingsmourne (starts at 4:47) 4th: 6:45 - 2nd intermission 5th: 8:45 - P3 at some point Group B (2nd kingsmourne group) WS timings 1st: 0:00 - on pull 2nd 2:00 - 15 sec into 2nd P1 kingsmourne (starts at 1:45) 3rd: 4:00 - towards end of 1st intermission 4th : 6:00 - 15 sec into 2nd P2 kingsmourne (starts at 5:45) 5th: 8:00 - P3 whenever Like I generally use barbed shot twice before WS, but if you're group B you should prob WS off the bat then do your regular opener so there's no delay Also prob a good idea to not use IQDs on this one, or if you do set your keybinds so you don't IQD until the 2nd cast (edited)
