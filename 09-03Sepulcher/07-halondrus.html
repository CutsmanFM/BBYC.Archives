Cutsman 29-Jul-22 08:55 PM
Someone asked me about why we're doing the 3rd group during the 2nd movement. The short answer is dropping a bomb puts you on a 40 sec CD, and you can only hold a bomb for 30 sec. That means the bomb has to sit on the floor for 10 sec for every pickup, which is ok during the first move since it is a 60 sec move, but the 2nd move lasts 1:24 which means it would have to sit on the floor mid movement and that's dangerous. I put a more detailed explanation here with timings. Not required reading at all, but in case you're curious: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1KJJzteU3I4LZSmF3baVmcgcGb7bHoIh7Gew_nyrbgRI/edit?usp=sharing (edited)

I also had some questions about the differences in potioning. That was to balance dps in each phase. Many of us are using the 5 minute pantheon trinket, because it lines up really well with this fight. However that also means there is a shortage of dps in phase 2 (as you could tell from prog). When we beat the reclaim, 3:00 classes will not have their stuff line up very well with that phase. I was deliberate in who should be potioning in P2 based on your CD timing (edited)
